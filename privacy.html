Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 4, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Anchored is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Email address for account creation and authentication
  • Notes Data: Your notes are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our secure servers if you choose premium sync
  • Usage Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve our service (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Anchored service
  • Sync your notes across devices (premium feature)
  • Send important service updates
  • Improve our service through anonymized analytics

3. Data Security

Your privacy is our priority:

  • Local-First: All notes are stored locally on your device first
  • End-to-End Encryption: Premium sync uses client-side encryption - we cannot read your notes
  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Your encrypted notes are unreadable by our servers
  • Secure Infrastructure: We use industry-standard security practices

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With service providers who help us operate (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your notes
  • Opt out of analytics

6. Browser Extension

Our browser extension:

  • Only accesses websites when you actively use it
  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Stores data locally in your browser
  • Only syncs data if you enable premium features

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Basic analytics (anonymized)

We do not use tracking cookies or sell data to advertisers.

8. Children's Privacy

Anchored is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: info@anchored.site
